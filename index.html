Corey Trujillo

Department of Mechanical Engineering
University of Colorado Boulder

I am a graduate reasearcher at the University of Colorado, Boulder working with Daven Henze. Using WRF-Chem and inverse modeling techniques, the goal of my research is to better understand the emissions from wildfires.

Prior to joining CU Boulder, I received my MS working with Miles Greiner and Mustafa Hadj-Nacer at the University of Nevada, Reno where I studied heat transfer in used nuclear fuel storage casks.

I received my bachelor's degrees from the University of Portland in 2014.
